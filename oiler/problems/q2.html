2. Pacific time

Timestamps on the west coast of USA can be written as mm/dd/yyyy hh:mm:ss in local “Pacific time”, for example:

10/15/2020 21:37:00
02/01/2021 00:00:00

Converting these example timestamps to unix time gives the following result:

1602823020
1612166400
